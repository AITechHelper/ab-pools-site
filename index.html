FAQ

Questions We Hear All the Time

Have a question not listed here? Call or text Chase directly — you'll always get a real answer from the owner.

How often should I have my pool cleaned?▾
Most residential pools benefit from weekly service during swim season. This keeps water chemistry balanced, prevents algae, and ensures your equipment runs efficiently all summer long.
When should I winterize my pool?▾
In the Tulsa area, we recommend winterizing before temperatures consistently drop below freezing — typically mid to late November. Proper winterizing protects your plumbing and equipment from freeze damage.
Do you handle pool repairs same day?▾
We do our best to respond quickly to repair calls. Many equipment repairs can be handled same day or next day, especially for urgent issues. Call Chase directly at (918) 986-0747 for fastest response.
Can you detect if my pool has a leak?▾
Yes. We use professional leak detection methods to locate leaks in your pool shell, plumbing, and equipment. If you're losing more than an inch of water per day, give us a call — it's likely a leak.
Do you do pool inspections for home purchases?▾
Absolutely. A pre-purchase pool inspection can save you thousands by revealing problems before you close on a home. We provide a thorough assessment of the pool structure, equipment, and systems.
What brands of pumps and filters do you install?▾
We work with all major brands including Pentair, Hayward, Jandy, and more. We'll recommend the right equipment for your pool size and budget and handle the full installation.
Are you available for emergencies?▾
Yes — A&B Pools is available 24 hours. Pool emergencies happen at all hours and we're reachable directly. Call or text (918) 986-0747 any time.
